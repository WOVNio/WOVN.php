So Good

Have you ever had a breakfast taco? I had some with my friend Jeff. He's so cool, just like Brad Pitt. Anyways, back to the tacos. The mecca of breakfast tacos is Austin, Texas. Eating a breakfast taco, for breakfast, is standard fare for Austinites. Though they eat them at all times of the day as well. One of the most famous places to get breakfast tacos is a little place called Juan In a Million. Don't go without expecting to wait to be seated! This place is always packed and their signature dish, the Don Juan, never disappoints. Stop by some time if you get the chance!
